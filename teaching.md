## Teaching 

CERMICS is involved in teaching at Ecole des Ponts, both for the <a href="http://www.enpc.fr/en/year-1">first year</a> (undergraduate) training, as well as in the <a href="http://www.enpc.fr/en/applied-mathematics-and-computer-science">Department of mathematical engineering</a> (graduate studies).

Researchers at CERMICS are also involved in teaching at <a href="https://portail.polytechnique.edu/mathematiquesappliquees/fr">Ecole Polytechnique</a> and in several masters in mathematics, in particular


 - <a href="https://www.ljll.math.upmc.fr/MathModel/">Mathematics for modeling</a> (Sorbonne Université)
 - <a href="http://www.enpc.fr/master-mathematiques-de-la-finance-et-des-donnees-mfd">Mathematics for finance and data</a> (with UGE)
 - <a href="https://sciences.sorbonne-universite.fr/formation-sciences/masters/master-mathematiques-et-applications/m2-parcours-probabilites-et-modeles">Probability and random models</a> (Sorbonne Université)
 - <a href="http://uma.ensta-paristech.fr/mpro/master2">Operations Research</a> (with various partners)
